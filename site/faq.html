FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

How far in advance should we book?

For weddings and bar/bat mitzvahs, we recommend booking 6 to 12 months in advance to secure your preferred date. For smaller events and social gatherings, 2 to 3 months is typically sufficient. Popular dates fill quickly, so earlier is always better. Contact us as soon as you have a tentative date in mind.

What is your service area?

We serve Boston's North Shore and Greater Boston, including Swampscott, Marblehead, Salem, Beverly, Danvers, Gloucester, Rockport, Manchester-by-the-Sea, Ipswich, Newburyport, Lynn, Peabody, and surrounding areas. We are experienced with a wide variety of venues throughout the region.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes, we offer tastings for booked clients. Once you've reserved your date, we'll schedule a tasting session so you can sample menu options and fine-tune every detail to your preferences. It's one of the most enjoyable parts of the planning process.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. We accommodate kosher, halal, gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, and allergy-specific dietary needs. Our entire team — including Bruce, Tammy, and Richie — is certified in Food Allergy Awareness. We take dietary requirements seriously and work closely with clients to ensure every guest is safely and deliciously accommodated.

What is your minimum guest count?

There is no strict minimum guest count. We are flexible and have experience with everything from intimate dinners for a handful of guests to large-scale events for up to 3,000 people. Every event receives the same level of care and attention.

Do you provide bar service?

Yes, we offer full bar service including custom cocktail menus, curated wine and beer selections, and non-alcoholic options. Our bartenders are experienced professionals who add to the atmosphere of your event.

What is included in your catering packages?

Every package is custom-designed to fit your specific event. Typically this includes food preparation and service, professional service staff, complete setup and breakdown, and event coordination. Linens, table settings, rental coordination, and additional event planning services are also available. We'll outline everything in your custom proposal.

Can you cater outdoor or tented events?

Yes. We bring fully equipped field kitchens to any location, even where no water or electricity exists. We have catered tented events for up to 3,000 guests. From backyard celebrations to oceanside galas, our team delivers the same quality you'd expect from a top indoor venue.

Do you offer event planning beyond catering?

Yes. We offer full event coordination including linens, table settings, rental coordination, design consultation, and day-of management. Our goal is for you to be a guest at your own party — we handle the logistics so you can enjoy every moment.

How does pricing work?

Every event receives a custom quote based on your menu selections, guest count, service level, and venue requirements. We don't use rigid packages — your proposal is tailored specifically to your event. Contact us to discuss your needs and we'll provide a detailed, transparent proposal.

Can you handle events with both adult and children's menus?

Yes, this is one of our specialties. We are especially experienced with bar and bat mitzvahs where separate kids' and adult menus are essential. We create fun, age-appropriate options that kids love alongside sophisticated adult fare that impresses even the most discerning guests.

What makes The Party Specialist different from other caterers?

Over 45 years of experience. The same core team working together since 1983 and 1984. A "Say Yes" philosophy that means no request is too creative or ambitious. Full Food Allergy Awareness certification for the entire team. And a track record of clients who come back event after event, decade after decade. We treat every party as though it were the most important event we have ever worked on.

Do you provide desserts and cakes?

Yes. We offer full dessert service including custom dessert tables, signature sundae bars, and a wide range of sweet options to complement your event. Desserts are designed to match your event's theme and style.

Can you work with our venue or private home?

Yes. We cater at any venue including private homes, synagogues, country clubs, hotels, and unique spaces. Our field kitchen capability means we can set up virtually anywhere. We have extensive experience with venues throughout the North Shore and Greater Boston.

How do we get started?

Call us at (781) 592-0988 or fill out the contact form on our website. We'll discuss your event details, your vision, and your budget, and then create a custom proposal tailored to you. The sooner you reach out, the more likely we can secure your preferred date.
