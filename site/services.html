Full-Service Catering & Events

Every Event, Handled

From intimate dinners to grand galas, The Party Specialist handles all kinds of events including weddings, corporate events, bar and bat mitzvahs and everything in between. Every menu is custom-designed. Every detail is considered.

Weddings

Your wedding day deserves perfection. We provide custom-designed menus tailored to your tastes and theme, full bar service, signature desserts, professional server staff, and seamless day-of coordination. From the cocktail hour to the last dance, every detail is handled.

Bar & Bat Mitzvahs

Our specialty, backed by decades of glowing testimonials. We create themed menus that excite kids and impress adults, with separate kids' and adult menus, interactive food stations, and creative presentations that make the celebration unforgettable.

Corporate Events

Professional service for any scale, from board dinners to company-wide celebrations. We understand the importance of flawless execution in a corporate setting and deliver polished, on-time service that reflects well on your organization.

Social Events

Birthdays, anniversaries, galas, holiday parties, retirement celebrations, and everything in between. No matter the occasion, we create a dining experience that your guests will talk about long after the event is over.

Outdoor & Tented Events

We bring fully equipped field kitchens to any location, even where no water or electricity exists. From backyard tents to oceanside celebrations, we cater outdoor events for up to 3,000 guests with the same quality you'd expect from a top venue.

Full Event Planning

We go far beyond catering. The Party Specialist offers complete event coordination including linens, table settings, rental coordination, design consultation, and day-of management. Be a guest at your own party while we handle everything.
